# AssetIngester
Toolset to jump from Maya or Blender DCC to Houdini USD

## Overview
**AssetIngester** is a streamlined toolset designed for modeling, texture artists, and generalists who want to quickly export their work from Maya or Blender into Houdini Solaris without the technical hassle.

## Features
- **One-Click Export**: Simple shelf tool integration in Maya and Blender
- **Automatic USD Generation**: Launches Houdini and creates production-ready USD files
- **Automated Turntables**: 
    - Modeling turntable with wireframe overlay
    - Look development turntable for shader review
- **Built-in Compositing**: Automatic comp generation in Houdini
- **Pipeline Integration**: Direct publishing to Discord or ShotGrid

## How It Works
1. Select your asset in Maya or Blender
2. Click the AssetIngester shelf tool
3. Houdini launches automatically and generates the USD
4. Review your turntables and comp
5. Publish directly to the team via Discord or ShotGrid
